ABOUT BUSES





IFTM UNIVERSITY was granted University status by the UP goverment vide IFTM University Act 24 of 2010. It has been pioneer in bringing technical and professionals education to the city of Moradabad in 1996.

The IFTM UNIVERSITY provided its own FREE BUS SERVICES for the wellfare of their students.There are 90 buses offered by the University for the different places such as RAMPUR,MILAK,SAMBHAL,THAKURDWARA,AMROHA,CANDAUSI,GWA etc.
The University allow the seperate buses for girls and boys in the last year for every stopage.

The buses have all safety equipments such as fire extension and fearsted kit for the wellfare of the students.
